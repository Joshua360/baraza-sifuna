About Me

Hi, I am Sifuna David. Armed with a diverse skill set, a technical background in crop maintenance and management, and a passionate commitment to support the attainment of food security in Africa, I have spent the last half a decade designing, implementing and managing systems, processes and procedures in fast-paced, demanding and performance-based agricultural environments; with a focus on enhancing both the quality and quantity of agricultural products, specifically fruits and vegetables.

I have honed skills in the areas of agricultural development, farmer training & mobilization, regulatory compliance, farm mapping, quality control and management, integrated pest management, field services, sales & marketing, nursery management, agricultural research and trend analysis, and seedling production. Over the course of my career, I have built an effective blend of leadership, business, and people management skills as well as expertise in agricultural risk monitoring, resource management, performance management, project management, relationship management, strategic business planning & negotiation.

I have a strong commercial and market orientation, coupled with an ability to utilise available technology to give maximum benefit to stakeholders, in terms of profitability, productivity and even sustainability. I am a person of high integrity, energy and enthusiasm, who encourages and respects creativity in others, always being practical and results-focused. I am an initiator, both in thinking and in relationships with well-developed interpersonal skills and a demonstrable ability to project a positive image and communicate articulately, professionally and effectively through oral and written mediums with diverse groups of individuals. I am renowned as a smart and innovative professional with a strong work ethic, capable of working positively and constructively even in the most pressurized environments. I am highly adaptable to new work cultures and rapidly gains skills and knowledge required to perform a role to the highest standard. I am passionate about making the world a better place, providing quality service and creating a positive impact in the life of others.
